# AI Capability Projections: Detailed Analysis

**Analysis Source:** AGI Timeline Forensics
**Methodology:** Capability projection, trend analysis
**Confidence Level:** Variable by projection

---

## Executive Summary

AI capabilities are projected to progress from current near-human levels to supra-human across most domains within the decade. The trajectory shows accelerating capability growth with alignment research lagging behind.

---

## Current State Assessment

### Language Understanding
- **Status:** Near-human
- **Gap:** Nuance, context, long-horizon coherence
- **Timeline to human-level:** 1-2 years

### Reasoning
- **Status:** Below human
- **Gap:** Multi-step logic, counterfactual reasoning
- **Timeline to human-level:** 3-4 years

### Scientific Discovery
- **Status:** Narrow domains only
- **Gap:** Hypothesis generation, experiment design
- **Timeline to broad capability:** 5-6 years

### Self-Improvement
- **Status:** Assisted only
- **Gap:** Autonomous architecture modification
- **Timeline to autonomous:** 4-5 years
