# Climate Analysis: Full Details

**Sector:** Climate Change
**Analysis Source:** Seer 5.0
**Confidence Level:** 0.90

## Executive Summary

Global temperature will reach +2.5°C above pre-industrial baseline by Year 10 (2035). This represents a fixed point—the atmospheric carbon is already sufficient to lock in this trajectory. What happens in the next 10 years determines whether we stabilize or continue acceleration.
